## 🚀 At a Glance
Kutools for Windows is a productivity toolkit from ExtendOffice that extends Microsoft Office with hundreds of practical tools and one-click utilities. It installs as an add-in and places its own tabs on the Office ribbon, so the fiddly, repetitive jobs across your spreadsheets, mail, and documents turn into single clicks. Three main products make up the suite — Kutools for Excel, Kutools for Outlook, and Kutools for Word — and Kutools for Excel is the most popular thanks to its huge library of features. It is a paid product with a free trial for evaluation.

## ✨ What You Get
| Component | What it adds |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Kutools for Excel** | Hundreds of utilities for batch operations, merging and splitting workbooks, combining rows and columns, advanced find and replace, and one-click cleanup of blanks, duplicates, and formatting. |
| **Kutools for Outlook** | Bulk email tools, one-click replies with attachments, automatic CC/BCC rules, and faster sorting, forwarding, and inbox management. |
| **Kutools for Word** | Batch document utilities that add or remove content across many files, convert tables and images, and clean up formatting in large documents. |
| **One-click utilities** | Multi-step Office chores compressed into single ribbon buttons, cutting the time and errors of doing them manually. |
| **Ribbon integration** | Installs as a native add-in with dedicated tabs inside Excel, Outlook, and Word, so every tool stays close to your work. |

## 🎯 Quick Start
1. Close any open Office apps so the add-in registers correctly during installation.
2. Open Excel, Outlook, or Word and spot the Kutools and Kutools Plus tabs on the ribbon.
3. Browse the grouped utilities and hover over a button to read what it does.
4. Run a simple one-click task first, like deleting blank rows or merging several files.
5. Start your free trial or apply your license when prompted to unlock the full toolkit.

## ❓ FAQ
**Is Kutools free?**
Kutools is a paid product that includes a free trial for evaluation. The trial lets you use the full range of tools before buying; there is no free full copy and nothing to bypass — you just download the trial.

**What is Kutools for Excel?**
Kutools for Excel is the most popular product in the suite: an add-in that installs hundreds of extra features into Excel — batch operations, merging, advanced find, one-click cleanup and more — reachable from a dedicated ribbon tab.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
Kutools for Windows runs on current 64-bit versions of Windows, including Windows 10 and Windows 11, with a supported copy of Microsoft Office or Microsoft 365 installed.

**Is my data secure?**
Kutools runs locally as an add-in inside your own Office apps, so your documents and mail stay on your machine. Refer to the resources that ship with the app for complete details on data handling.
